About

Harrison Renewable Energy

Based in New York, Harrison Renewable Energy provides liquidity for renewable energy credits, carbon credits, and power purchase agreements. We take principle in every REC and carbon credit transaction, and we broker PPAs.

We're active in compliance and voluntary REC markets in the following registries: PJM GATS, NEPOOL GIS, NCRETS, MIRECS, MRETS, ERCOT, WREGIS, and NAR. We're active in the following carbon offset markets: VCS, ACR, CAR, and GS.

Additionally, we offer professional research services to help you navigate these complex markets.

Despite the nature of trading and the sometimes opaque markets we work in, we emphasize honesty and forthrightness in every transaction.

Services

HRE covers all voluntary and compliance markets.

Brokerage

We're an introducing broker for renewable energy power purchase agreements, matching developers with traditional and corporate offtakers. We’re also partnered with a power trading company to help generators manage merchant, basis, and volumetric risk.

Trading

HRE uses it's own capital to buy and sell RECs and carbon credits direct. Our aim is to beat our competition on price and close a high number of trades for a reasonable margin. We're a lean shop, and we’re volume driven; trading with us will be quick and efficient every time.

Research

REC, carbon credit, and PPA markets involve many details and can be complex areas to navigate. We're well versed in all of these markets, and we're happy to share what we've learned with you. HRE provides this research as a complementary service to all clients.
